Adolf Hitler(1889-1945)

Adolf Hitler, head of the Nazi Party (from 1920/21) . also, chancellor (Kanzler) and Führer of Germany (1933–45). He was chancellor from January 30, 1933, and, after President Paul von Hindenburg's passing, expected the twin titles of Führer and chancellor (August 2, 1934).
