Pleistocene Epoch

The Pleistocene Epoch is typically defined as the time period that began about 2.6 million years ago and lasted until about 11,700 years ago. The is also the time period when the last ice age occurs. There have been at least five major ice ages in the 4.6 billion years since the Earth was formed. Homo sapiens first time evolved during this time period.
