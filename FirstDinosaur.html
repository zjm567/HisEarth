The First Dinosaur

The early Triassic was dominated by mammal-like reptiles such as Lystrosaurus. Lystrosaurus was small and later evolved larger and becomes the first dinosaur named Plateosaurus. Plateosaurus is a large plant eating dinosaur that's about 9 meters length. Plateosaurus walked on all four of his legs and is a relative of the much larger sauropods.
